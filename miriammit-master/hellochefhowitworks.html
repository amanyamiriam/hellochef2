How it works

Simplify home cooking 4 easy steps.

Select your box

Whether you are cooking for two or feeding a family, we have flexible box sizes to fit your every need


Choose your recipes

With your size determined you can now select your recipes for the 20 delicious dishes on our menu. Take control of your meal planning and look out for brand-new recipes next week !


Receive your delivery

choose from 5 delivery days to suit your schedule and stand by for hello chef box to arrive. If you need to change your delivery day or skip a week, you can do so easily in your account setting.

Cook,eat and enjoy!

Once your box has been delivered with your choosen recipes and pre-packed ingredient,all that is left to dois for you to cook! enjoy a fuss-free cooking experience with our simple and delicious recipes
